Our Story

At SpiceHub, we are passionate about bringing you the most authentic Hyderabadi Biryani experience. Founded by master chef Ustad Mahmood Ali, who learned the art from the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, our restaurant carries forward a 200-year-old tradition.

Each biryani is meticulously prepared using the traditional Dum cooking method, where aromatic basmati rice and perfectly spiced meat are slow-cooked together in sealed pots, allowing the flavors to meld into pure perfection.

From the fragrant Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani to the delicate Chicken Dum Biryani, every dish is a celebration of Nizami culinary heritage, served with love and authenticity.
